About Us

Established in 2020, Five Eyes Consulting is a fast-growing Information Security and Technology consulting firm based in Australia. With a workforce that has over 20 years of experience specialising in Cyber Security, Information Technology and Programme and Project Management, we partner with public and private sector organisations of all sizes to deliver secure and quality solutions and outcomes.

With people, technology and innovation at the forefront, we have created an atmosphere where self-development and creativity are supported and encouraged, enabling us to deliver quality outcomes for not only our communities but the Five Eyes Nations. By collaborating with our experienced team we help you utilise the right technology empowering your response to changing environments and threats.

Our Purpose

Whether we are advising, delivering, or creating, our purpose is to help our communities solve their most complex information security challenges through our people, technology, and innovation.

Our Vision

We aim to be the standard of excellence for Information Security and employer choice for security professionals.

Our Mission

We create information security solutions that are secured by design and meet our clients desired outcomes.
