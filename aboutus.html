Our Story

We are a fast growing information security consultancy with people, technology and innovation at the centre. Follow our journey from the beginning with our founders Rafal and Janstan.

  1. 2020 – Establishing Five Eyes Consulting

    Five Eyes Consulting was founded in 2020 by Rafal Lukasiewicz and Janstan Espinosa, bringing together an average of over 20 years of industry experience. Drawing from their past experience and expertise in the Security (Cyber and Physical) and Information Technology sectors, they had a vision for Five Eyes Consulting to be the standard of excellence for information security across the Five Eyes nations.

  2. 2021 – Building the Five Eyes Consulting Brand

    2021 was all about succession planning for our organisation. Despite the COVID-19 pandemic lockdowns and restrictions in 2021, Five Eyes Consulting continued to grow and put effort into developing industry relationships and partnerships. During this time, we became an approved DISP member, partnered with multiple technology companies including Microsoft, Amazon, Cisco, Fortinet, Tanium and Tenable and developed relationships with multiple consultancies ranging in size. This enabled us to create change for multiple clients and create greater opportunities for our organisation and people.

  3. 2022 – Growing the Five Eyes Consulting Team

    In 2022, Five Eyes Consulting plans to continue to strengthen their presence in the market, developing information security capability and building the team’s expertise
