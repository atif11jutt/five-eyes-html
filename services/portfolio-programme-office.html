What is Portfolio, Programme & Project Management Office Services?

PMO services helps you improve the management of your projects and programmes by standardising processes and improving efficiency

Why Five Eyes?

Five Eyes Consulting provides PMO services including reviews of existing processes, design and implementation of frameworks and methodologies, tools and templates, data gathering and analysis, project and program controls, benefits realisation, risk and issues management, master scheduling, stakeholder engagement, secretariat, and executive reporting
