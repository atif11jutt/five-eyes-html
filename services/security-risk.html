Security Risk Management

What is Security Risk Management?

Risk management services helps your organisation identify, assess and control threats as well as minimise, monitor and control the probability or impact of unfortunate events to maximize the realisation of opportunities.

Why Five Eyes?

Five Eyes Consulting works on various programmes and projects ranging in complexity and size mitigating information security risks. Our Risk Specialists provide pragmatic, detailed and actionable risk management services to help manage the risk trade-off to enable informed decision-making. This includes developing risk plans (Security Risk Management Plans), performing risk management for your projects and programmes and performing independent risk assessments on your information assets, technologies and threats.
